words

Linguistic unit, generally endowed with meaning, which is separated from the others by potential breaks in pronunciation and blank spaces in writing.

collage art

Artistic technique that consists of pasting different images on a canvas or paper. The term is applied mainly to painting, but by extension it can refer to any other artistic manifestation, such as music, cinema, literature or video clips. It comes from the French coller, which means to paste.
